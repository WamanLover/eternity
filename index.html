The woman behind the planning

Mrs Planner is a visionary event planner whose creativity, precision, and passion elevate every occasion into an unforgettable experience. Born in Cape Town and educated in Event Management and Hospitality at the University of Johannesburg, she began her career orchestrating boutique corporate events and luxury private celebrations before founding Eternity, where she now curates bespoke experiences across South Africa, from Johannesburg and Pretoria to the breathtaking landscapes of the Winelands and safari destinations. Over the past decade, she has designed and executed hundreds of events, including immersive brand activations, large-scale galas, intimate milestone celebrations, and luxury weddings, earning recognition from clients both locally and internationally. Beyond planning, she is an entrepreneur, mentor, and creative thought leader, dedicated to empowering emerging talent in the event industry through workshops and mentorship programs. Known for her ability to blend grand spectacle with meticulous attention to detail, she ensures that every element—from décor and entertainment to logistics—is thoughtfully orchestrated, leaving guests with lasting memories. Her philosophy centers on storytelling through events, creating experiences that reflect the unique personality and vision of each client while maintaining the highest standards of elegance and sophistication. Whether transforming historic venues into whimsical dreamscapes or designing seamless corporate functions, Mrs Planner’s signature touch turns visions into reality and consistently sets a benchmark for excellence in the world of event planning.
